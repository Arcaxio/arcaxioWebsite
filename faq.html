FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are the fees I should expect?

    We are unable to provide you a realistic range, because it could range anywhere from RM50 to RM1000. Please contact us so we can give you an estimate

  • Do I need to provide the certificates for my pet?

    It is not required. However, if your pet has any existing medical conditions, please bring the any relevant certificates.

  • How can I change my appointment details?

    You can cancel your appointment first, and then resubmit your new appointment after.

  • How can I cancel my appointment?

    You can reply "Cancel Appointment" in your email, and our system will automatically cancel the appointment.

  • I did not receive an email after submitting my appointment

    Please check that you've entered the correct email address. Otherwise, you can contact us directly through email or phone.
