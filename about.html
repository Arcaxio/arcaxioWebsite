About Us

At PetCare Agency, we provide comprehensive and compassionate care for pets when their owners can't be there. As pet care experts, our sitters are certified in animal behavior, pet first aid, and catering to pets' unique needs. We offer a wide range of personalized services including daily walks, feedings, medication administration, and playtime right in your home. For anxious or special needs pets, we have calming care plans and exercises to keep them relaxed and enriched. We provide flexible booking options from occasional drop-ins to recurring daily visits to suit each client's needs. Our team covers all areas of the city and will collaborate with you to set up a customized care schedule. At PetCare Agency, your pets are family to us too. Contact us to learn more about our comprehensive pet sitting and pet care services.
